# How It Works

## The Three-Step Workflow

Notewright is built around a simple idea: your AI pen captures the raw material — Notewright helps you do something with it.

---

### Step 1 — Capture

Press record on your AI pen. Jot key points on paper while the device captures full audio. The Flowtica Scribe pen features a magnetic cap, FlowMark highlight button (press to bookmark important moments in real time), and writes with real ink on real paper.

**What you get:** A complete audio recording synced with your handwritten notes, plus FlowMark bookmarks at the moments that matter.

---

### Step 2 — Summarise

Once your session ends, AI processes the recording into a structured summary. Not a wall of text — a clean breakdown of key decisions, discussion themes, and takeaways. The Snap It feature converts your handwritten notes into editable text.

**What you get:** A formatted meeting summary, handwriting converted to text, and searchable voice recordings.

---

### Step 3 — Act

Turn your notes into outcomes. Action items are extracted automatically with owners and deadlines. Draft follow-up emails directly from meeting content. Search across all your recordings by voice.

**What you get:** A clear action list, ready-to-send follow-ups, and a searchable archive of every conversation.

---

## The Device

Notewright guides are optimised for the **Flowtica Scribe** AI pen, but the principles and workflows apply to any leading AI note-taking device.

**Key Flowtica Scribe Features:**
- FlowMark button — bookmark important moments during recording
- Magnetic cap — quick-draw design
- Real ink on real paper — no special paper required
- Multi-speaker separation — identifies up to 15 distinct voices
- Snap It — converts handwriting to editable digital text
- Voice search — find any moment across all recordings
