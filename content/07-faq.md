**What is Notewright?**
Notewright is an education brand that helps professionals get real value from AI note-taking pens. We create guides, workflows, and templates that turn meeting recordings into structured notes, clear action items, and professional follow-ups.

---

**What devices does Notewright work with?**
Our guides are optimised for the Flowtica Scribe AI pen, but the principles, workflows, and templates apply to any leading AI note-taking device. You don't need a specific pen to benefit from Notewright.

---

No. Notewright is an independent brand. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or partnered with Flowtica or any other device manufacturer. Our recommendations are based on professional use-case testing, not commercial relationships.

**Do I need a specific AI pen to use Notewright?**
No. While our content is optimised for the Flowtica Scribe, the workflows and templates are designed to work with any AI note-taking device that records audio and creates transcripts.

**Is my data safe?**
Notewright does not access, store, or process your recordings or notes. Our products are educational guides and templates that you use alongside your own device and its companion app. For information about how your device handles data, refer to your device manufacturer's privacy policy.
