# Features

## Everything your AI pen can do — unlocked

Most people use 10% of their AI pen's capability. Notewright helps you use the other 90%.

---

### AI Meeting Summaries
Automatic structured summaries after every meeting — key decisions, themes, and takeaways organised clearly. No more scrolling through raw transcripts.

### FlowMark Bookmarking
Press the FlowMark button during a meeting to bookmark critical moments. Jump straight to the parts that matter when you review later.

### Snap It — Handwriting to Text
Convert handwritten notes into editable digital text instantly. Your pen-and-paper notes become searchable, shareable, and actionable.

### Voice Search
Search across all your recordings by keyword or phrase. Find that one comment from last Tuesday's meeting in seconds.

### Automatic Action Items
AI extracts action items from your meetings with suggested owners and deadlines. Export directly to your task manager.

### Multi-Speaker Separation
Identifies and labels up to 15 distinct speakers in a recording. Know exactly who said what, even in crowded meetings.

### Follow-Up Email Drafting
Draft follow-up emails directly from meeting content. AI pulls key points and action items into a ready-to-send format.

### Multi-Language Support
Record and transcribe in multiple languages. Ideal for international teams and multilingual meetings.
