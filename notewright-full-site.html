How It Works

Notewright is built around a simple idea: your AI pen captures the raw material — Notewright helps you do something with it.

1

Capture

Press record on your AI pen. Jot key points on paper while the device captures full audio. The Flowtica Scribe pen features a magnetic cap, FlowMark highlight button, and writes with real ink on real paper.

You get: Complete audio recording synced with handwritten notes, plus FlowMark bookmarks at the moments that matter.

2

Summarise

AI processes the recording into a structured summary. Not a wall of text — a clean breakdown of key decisions, discussion themes, and takeaways. Snap It converts handwriting to editable text.

You get: Formatted meeting summary, searchable voice recordings, and handwriting converted to text.

3

Act

Turn notes into outcomes. Action items are extracted automatically with owners and deadlines. Draft follow-up emails from meeting content. Search across all recordings by voice.

You get: A clear action list, ready-to-send follow-ups, and a searchable archive of every conversation.
