# Notewright Brand Guide

## Brand Name
**Notewright** (one word, capital N)

## Tagline
Turn every conversation into action

## Logo
**Official logo:** `brand/notewright-logo-v5-system-1.svg` — faceted nib + circuitry mark

Variants included in the brand sheet:
- **Primary Logo** — navy mark + navy text on light backgrounds
- **On Dark** — light mark + white text on dark/navy backgrounds (used in nav bar)
- **The Mark** — standalone icon (no text)
- **App / Social Icon** — rounded square badge versions (light, grey, dark)

## Colours (updated v5)
- **Navy:** #16263F
- **Navy mid:** #21395C
- **Gold:** #E2B53C
- **Gold deep:** #BC8A1C
- **Light:** #F4F6FA

*Legacy website palette (pending full migration):*
- *Accent Teal:* #2A9D8F
- *Light Grey:* #F5F5F5

## Typography
- **Headings:** Poppins (Bold 700)
- **Body:** Inter or clean sans-serif, 16px minimum

## Tone of Voice
- Professional but approachable
- Clear and direct
- Confident without being pushy
- Educational, not salesy

## Key Messages
1. The real value isn't in the device — it's in what you do with the notes.
2. Most people use 10% of their AI pen's capability. Notewright helps you use the other 90%.
3. Capture → Summarise → Act

## Independence Statement
*Notewright is independent and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Flowtica.*

This statement must appear on every page that mentions Flowtica or any device manufacturer.

## CTA Rules
- All call-to-action buttons say "Join the Waitlist"
- No "Buy Now", "Add to Cart", or pricing with checkout
- All CTAs link to the waitlist signup form (email-only)
